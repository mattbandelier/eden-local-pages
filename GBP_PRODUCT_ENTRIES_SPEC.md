# GBP Product Entries Spec

## Goal

Create 13 Google Business Profile Product entries that point visitors from Eden Health Club's Google profile to the new landing-page service hubs.

Primary outcome: start sending local, high-intent organic traffic from Google Business Profile to `landing.edenhealthclubs.com`.

## Non-Goals

- Do not edit the website.
- Do not change Google Ads, GTM, GA4, or GHL.
- Do not add appointment links unless the GBP UI specifically requires a button URL.
- Do not use drug-name or prescription-specific claims in product descriptions.
- Do not make outcome guarantees.

## Global Settings

Use these defaults for every Product entry:

- Business Profile: Eden Health Club
- Product category: use the category listed per entry below
- Button: `Learn more`
- Price: leave blank unless the entry says to add an exact price
- URL format:

```text
https://landing.edenhealthclubs.com/{service-slug}/?utm_source=google_business_profile&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gbp_products&utm_content={service-slug}
```

## GBP UI Workflow

1. Open the Eden Health Club Google Business Profile manager.
2. Go to `Edit products`.
3. Click `Add product`.
4. Add the product name, category, optional price, description, and `Learn more` URL from the table below.
5. Save.
6. Repeat for all 13 services.
7. After all entries are saved, open the public Google profile and spot-check at least 3 product links.

## Notes

- Product entries may take time to appear publicly.
- Google may lightly rewrite or truncate descriptions.
- If Google rejects an entry, shorten the description first. Do not add stronger medical claims to force approval.
- If a service has sensitive medical language, keep the Product entry consult-focused and let the landing page provide the full clinical context.
